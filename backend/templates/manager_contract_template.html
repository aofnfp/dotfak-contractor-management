4. Compensation

The Manager shall be compensated at a flat rate of ${{ "%.2f"|format(flat_hourly_rate) }} USD per hour for all hours worked by the managed contractor as reported on the Client's payroll records.

Manager compensation is calculated as: Total Staff Hours Worked x Flat Hourly Rate. No overtime multipliers, bonuses, or additional premiums shall apply to the Manager's compensation regardless of the nature of the staff contractor's hours (including overtime, holiday, or other premium hours).

Compensation under this Agreement is fixed and non-negotiable for the duration of this Agreement.

5. Payment Terms

Payments to the Manager shall be processed via direct bank deposit within five (5) business days following the Company's receipt of corresponding payment from the Client. The Manager acknowledges that the Company's payment obligations are contingent upon the Company's receipt of funds from the Client.

The Manager is responsible for providing accurate and current banking information to the Company for payment processing.

6. Equipment and Device Management

The Manager shall be responsible for managing all equipment and devices assigned to the managed contractor, including but not limited to:

  • Receiving equipment from the Company and ensuring proper delivery to the managed contractor;
  • Maintaining accurate records of device serial numbers, credentials, and status;
  • Ensuring timely return of all equipment upon contractor termination or reassignment;
  • Reporting any loss, damage, or security concerns regarding equipment to the Company within twenty-four (24) hours.

The Manager shall bear responsibility for any equipment lost or damaged while under their custody or the custody of the managed contractor.

7. Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

The Manager acknowledges that during the course of this engagement, they may have access to confidential and proprietary information belonging to the Company, the Client, or the managed contractor. The Manager agrees to:

  • Not disclose any Confidential Information to unauthorized parties;
  • Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing management services;
  • Not disclose the managed contractor's compensation, earnings, or financial details to any third party;
  • Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon termination.

This confidentiality obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years.

8. Data Privacy and Security

The Manager shall handle all data, including device credentials and contractor information, in accordance with applicable privacy and data protection laws. The Manager agrees to store credentials securely and report any data breach within twenty-four (24) hours of discovery.

9. Independent Contractor Status

The Manager is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of the Company. The Manager shall be solely responsible for all tax obligations in their jurisdiction of residence.

10. Non-Solicitation

During the term of this Agreement and for a period of twelve (12) months following its termination, the Manager shall not directly or indirectly solicit, recruit, or attempt to hire any contractor or employee engaged by the Company, or divert business from any client of the Company.

11. Liability and Indemnification

The Manager shall indemnify and hold harmless the Company from any claims, damages, or losses arising from the Manager's breach of this Agreement, negligence, or willful misconduct.

12. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement by providing thirty (30) days written notice. The Company may terminate immediately for cause, including material breach, misconduct, or failure to fulfill management responsibilities.

Upon termination, the Manager shall ensure all managed equipment is accounted for and returned, and all Confidential Information is returned or destroyed.
